# JobFit AI

JobFit AI analyzes job postings against your resume and scores how well you match — with actionable feedback on skills, seniority fit, and CV improvements.

## What you can do

- **Upload CVs** (PDF/DOCX) and run match analyses against pasted job text or URLs
- **Track history** with match scores, filters, and side-by-side comparison
- **Tailor bullets**, generate **cover letters**, and build **learning plans** from each report
- **Track applications** on a kanban board (Saved → Applied → Interview → Offer)
- **Re-score** after uploading a revised CV and see the match % delta

## Stack

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|------------|
