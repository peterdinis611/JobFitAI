## What JobFit AI does

- **Upload CVs** (PDF/DOCX) and score fit against any job posting
- **Track history** with filters, comparison, and export
- **Tailor bullets**, generate **cover letters**, build **learning plans**
- **Track applications** on a kanban board (Saved → Offer)
- **Re-score** after CV edits and see match % delta
